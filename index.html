diploma-crossfity - It's a 'classic' django based app which holds relations between coaches and athletes.

If you want to join 'crossfity' as a 'coach', first you need to be verified (admin reads your application form and grants you acces to registration form via e-mail and sms code. If you wish to register as an 'athlete' user all you need to do is to fill a athlete registration form corectly and send it.

As a 'coach' you can create custom WODs(Workout Of a Day) and schedule it in your training plan. You can also track progress of your subscribers and process data in many ways.
As an 'athlete' user You'll get something like workout journal. You can subscribe to coache's training plans, submit your scores and create personal WODs (which can not be distributed among others like coache's wods).
